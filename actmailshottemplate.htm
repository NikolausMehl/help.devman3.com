When sending a mailshot, using a template is ideal.  A template is saved onto the system and can be used over and over again.  It also enables you to personalise each email by inserting tags.  Any number of templates can be set up for use.  You may wish to setup your own template or you may contact Kidz Africa for assistance.

 

1.If more than one template is available you will need to choose which one you would like to use. If not, it will default to the basic email newsletter.  Customisation of this basic email template is a specialised area of the system, and you can contact us to assist you with this.

2.By clicking on the Edit in full screen mode button, you can change the content as well as the look of your newsletter.

 

Click here to view Full screen edit

 

3.To insert a To tag, position your cursor in the correct spot and click on To Tags. Choose the tag you wish to include in your email e.g.Person > SurnamP.
